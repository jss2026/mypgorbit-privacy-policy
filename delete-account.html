Delete Account – MyPGOrbit

This page explains how users of MyPGOrbit, developed by Ahalya Aura Creation, can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your MyPGOrbit account and associated data, please send an email to:

Email: ahalyaauracreation@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Registered email address or phone number
  • App name: MyPGOrbit
  • Request: Account deletion

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information (email / phone number)
  • PG, room, tenant, and rent-related data

Data retention

Account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of receiving a valid deletion request, unless retention is required by law.

After deletion, the data cannot be recovered.
